The most promising city in Brazil

A prominent city in the state, inside and outside the country. Located in the North of the State of RS, within the region known as the middle plateau, Marau has an area of more than 650 square kilometers.

Community

Colonized by Italian immigrants in 1904, it was transformed into a municipality on February 28, 1955. With a population of over 36,000, the companies located in Marau serve the most diverse sectors, including food, leather, poultry and swine farming equipment. , metal mechanics and the growing civil construction sector.

Shopping

It promotes the circulation of money in an economy and establishes important distribution channels between industry and the final consumer. Through demands to be more competitive, differentials are presented as a way to serve the consumer, many have evolved to the point of offering in the same establishment several distributions of essential items for human consumption.

Explore

Among the sights in the city of Marau and also in the rural area of the municipality, the following stand out: Redeemer Christ Church, Central Park, Lourdes Sanctuary, Wheat Railroad, and Big Stone Waterfall.

People
